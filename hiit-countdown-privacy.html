Privacy Policy for HIIT Countdown

This privacy policy describes how HIIT Countdown collects and uses data from users of our iPhone app. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains how we use and safeguard any data that we collect from you when you use our app.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from users of HIIT Countdown. However, our app does include AdMob ads and Google Analytics, which may collect certain non-personal information, such as device type, operating system, and app usage data. This information is collected solely for the purpose of providing targeted advertising and improving our app's functionality.

AdMob Ads

Our app includes AdMob ads, which are provided by Google. AdMob may use certain data, such as device type and app usage data, to provide targeted advertising. For more information on AdMob's privacy policy, please visit their website at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

Google Analytics

Our app also uses Google Analytics to track usage data and app performance. This information is collected anonymously and is used solely to improve our app's functionality. For more information on Google Analytics' privacy policy, please visit their website at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

Use of Data

We do not use any user data that we collect for any purposes. The only data that we collect is used by AdMob to display personalized ads to you.

Data Sharing

We do not share any user data that we collect with any third parties, except for AdMob, which may use the data to deliver personalized ads.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the data that we collect from you. However, no data transmission over the internet is completely secure, so we cannot guarantee the security of your data.

Updates to this Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time by posting a new version on our website. You should check this page occasionally to ensure that you are happy with any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy policy, please contact us at support@littlelioncreative.co.uk.

By using our app, you agree to the terms of this privacy policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use our app.
